The Madison Reunion was a "together again for the first time" gathering on June 14 - 16, 2018, at the (newly-renovated) Memorial Union and at venues on campus and around town.

The Reunion hosted 1000 people for three days of music, meet-ups and deep diving into topics of meaning and importance at the Conference on Madison in the 60's.

We are happy to announce the posting of videos of all panels from the conference – more than 40 hours capturing topics from war to comedy, music to history, journalism to theater and beyond.
